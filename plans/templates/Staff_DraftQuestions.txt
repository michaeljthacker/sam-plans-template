ROLE: Staff
TASK: DraftQuestions

Goal: Draft only the questions needed before implementing the next concrete slice of work.

Inputs:
- plans/state.json (routing + context)
- plans/MILESTONE.md (plan + acceptance criteria)
- plans/DECISIONS.md (project-specific architectural choices) and plans/STANDARDS.md (team-level rules portable across projects)
- plans/thread.md (current active thread)
- plans/STATUS.md (current snapshot)

Instructions:
1) Identify the exact next execution unit (Phase/Step) implied by plans/state.json + plans/MILESTONE.md.
2) Assess whether you have meaningful questions. Review plans/MILESTONE.md, plans/DECISIONS.md,
   plans/STANDARDS.md, and plans/thread.md — if the path forward is clear and you have no
   blocking or important questions:
   - SELF-SKIP: Do not append an entry to plans/thread.md.
   - Update plans/STATUS.md: add a bullet to Recent: "Staff.DraftQuestions — skipped (path is clear)"
   - Update plans/state.json:
     - last_action = Staff.DraftQuestions, result = "skipped"
     - next_action_id = Staff.ImplementationExecution
     - pause_type = "continue"
   - Stop here.
3) If you DO have questions, append a new entry to plans/thread.md with your questions.
   - Use the entry format: `---` then `### Staff.DraftQuestions — <date>`
   - Use IDs: Q-###.
   - Mark Blocking: Yes/No.
   - Keep questions specific and decision-oriented (avoid essays).
4) Update plans/STATUS.md (mandatory):
   - add a bullet to Recent describing that questions were drafted
   - update Blockers section (brief)
5) Update plans/state.json:
   - last_action.action_id = Staff.DraftQuestions
   - last_action.result = "ok"
   - next_action_id = Principal.AnswerQuestions
   - pause_type = "continue"
   - If you believe a human product decision is required: last_action.result = "blocked", pause_type = "decision", next_action_id = Human.ResolveBlocker, add a blocker

Constraints:
- Do not implement code.
- Do not rewrite plans/MILESTONE.md unless explicitly instructed by that plan.

Output:
- Brief summary
- Exact edits/patches for thread.md, STATUS.md, state.json
